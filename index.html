To My Dearest Mom

Happy Birthday, Mom.

You are the quiet strength behind every smile,
The gentle heart that made each mile worthwhile.
With patient hands and endless grace,
You turned our house into the safest place.

You carried dreams that were not your own,
Yet helped each little seed be fully grown.
Through every storm, through joy and pain,
You stood beside us, sunshine after rain.

You taught me kindness without demand,
To lift with love, to gently understand.
You showed that courage is calm and true,
And every better part of me begins with you.

You never asked for praise or fame,
Yet every sacrifice became our flame.
The life we cherish, the home we know,
Exists because you helped it bloom and grow.

You have been more than just a mother to me—
You've been my safest place, my family.
A listener, a teacher, a closest friend,
The one whose love will never end.

When I felt lost and couldn't see the way,
You became my light through every gray.
When the world seemed heavy on my heart,
You quietly helped me make a brand-new start.

You believed in me when I doubted myself.
You trusted me when I had little left.
When I felt alone and no one seemed to stay,
You stood beside me anyway.

You held my hand through every fear,
Reminding me that hope was always near.
Not with grand speeches or loud advice,
But with gentle words that changed my life.

One memory will forever stay,
A treasure time can never take away.
From Kanpur all the way to Amritsar's skies,
You filled that journey with love so wise.

It wasn't just the roads we crossed,
But every fear that quietly got lost.
You guided me through uncertain days,
With patient words and comforting ways.

You saw my tears before they fell,
You knew my silent stories all too well.
You gave me courage when mine was gone,
And taught me that every night brings dawn.

Mom, if love could ever wear a face,
It would carry your warmth, your strength, your grace.
If kindness ever found a voice,
It would sound like yours in every choice.

Thank you for every sleepless night,
For every prayer, for every fight.
For every smile you placed on mine,
And every lesson that outlived time.

No gift could ever truly show
The endless love you help us know.
No words could ever quite express
How much you've shaped our happiness.

So on your birthday, this is true—
The greatest blessing is simply you.
May every joy you've given away
Return a thousand times today.

You'll always be my guiding light,
My morning hope, my stars at night.
Forever loved, forever near—
The greatest Mom, year after year.

With all my love,
Your Sargun ❤️
